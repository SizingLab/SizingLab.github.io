Scientific interest groups

As the result of a scientific partnership intended to federate skills and resources to carry out a specific research program,the scientific interest groups (GIS) correspond to a simple contractual practice without reference to a legal and regulatory text.

For more details https://www.dgdr.cnrs.fr/daj/Archive%202017/partenariat/partenariat/Archives/structcontractuelles/gis1.htm
